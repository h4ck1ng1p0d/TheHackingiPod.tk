Big Leak at Amsterdam Schiphol

Nov 24th, 2021

BREAKING NEWS AMSTERDAM SCHIPHOL BIG SECURITY LEAK

Recently I read an article about this leak at the airport. Amsterdam Schiphol International Airport is the busiest airport in Europe and 3rd busiest airport In the world. Because the airport is so large they have a really large and advanced security but sometimes they have some holes.

Let explain what the leak is:

When your booking your flight you will receive a boarding pass with your flight information and if you checked in online (well, in Europe) you will receive a barcode or Qr-Code which you scan at the entry gates and then go through security.

Well, knowing that a Qr-Code just a simple algorithm with some plain text in it. Which is unencrypted and doesn’t have to be confirmed from a database or some other authentication. But when the hacker X (that’s how I’m gonna call him) developed an simple web based application to generate these codes. We can bypass the entry game without having to buy a ticket.

Flight Operator & Flight Number:i’m a frequent flyer and I always fly on KLM and always go on vacation to Italy and I know the flight number is 1643 or 1645 but enter any flight number you like.

Class and seat number:Because this ticket isn’t a real ticket you can enter any random class and seat number just have fun with it. Once you’re done you can scroll down and save.

The hacker X after doing something reconnaissance went to the new entry gates at Schiphol and placed the ticket or code on the scanner and the gates opened for him.

The leak right here shows that then entry gates at Schiphol aren’t connected to any authentication system that checks if the ticket is valid. But it’s just a simple scanner which is connected to a simple database and the only thing that happens is the credentials get saved and stored in a database.

Black hat hackers or Criminals can use this in 2 ways:

  • Travel under a fake name
  • Enter the airports duty free

After doing some research I found out that the government and other parties aren’t going to anything about it for a little while and I don’t know when they’re going to plan a fix.
