Facebook Taken Offline!

Oct 4th, 2021

Recently has Facebook reported to be taken offline for around 6 hours. Mister Zucks really sucked for a couple of hours. After the issues was fix zucks wrote on his profile an apology for the outage.

How could this happen? I’m not addicted to Facebook or the family of apps like most people are, but I have a couple of theories why Facebook has gone down for a couple of hours.

  • DDoS
  • ISP messed with the BGP
  • Hacker Group

DDoS The first thing I’m started thinking when I heard this was that someone with a large botnet used it to Denial of Service (Dos) Facebook, making the server overload and crash putting the site offline. Something like this is illegal BTW but it’s also super hard to trace back from where it came from because it’s a Distributed attack it’s coming from maybe all over the country or even the world.


What is a BGP Table? Before I explain what could’ve happened, let me explain what BGP is. BGP or Border Gateway Protocol is the protocol that runs the internet. These are used by ISP (internet service provider) use these to connect to other ISP which is the definition of the Internet is something is misconfigured or messed up you can literally put the internet offline.


BGP hack the second thought was about the BGP routing tables that I’ve learned from networking engineering courses. Maybe the ISP was maintaining the routing tables and maybe inputting a new route putting the site offline. This isn’t the first time this has happened over the past year multiple website such as: Facebook, Twitter and even YouTube were taken offline because the ISP was messing with BGP routing tables.

Another thing I was thinking is maybe someone successfully hacked an ISP around the world and added a route to the routing and messed up the configuration putting everything offline.


Hacker Group The last thing I can think about is that a group of hackers decided to attack Facebook and succeeded by putting the site and the hole family offline for 6 hours straight. But who knows Facebook has gone offline before and hopefully it happens again.
